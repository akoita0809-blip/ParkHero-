Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! New users get 3 free reservations to try ParkHero before committing to a plan.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel your subscription at any time with no hidden fees or penalties.

How do I pay?

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay for easy payments.

What is the per reservation fee?

A small service fee is added to each reservation. The exact amount depends on the parking provider.
